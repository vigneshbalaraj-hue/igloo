If your reel is not delivered, fails our quality check, or is rejected for any reason, you receive a 100% refund within 24 hours. See our Refund Policy for details.

Access during beta is limited and admitted in waves. When the current wave is full, you can join the waitlist and we will notify you as new spots open.

As Igloo matures, pricing tiers will expand to include longer reels, voice cloning, monthly subscriptions, and team accounts. Beta customers will be grandfathered into preferential pricing.
